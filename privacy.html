Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

At ToolZenHub, your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website and tools.

ToolZenHub is designed to work primarily in your browser. We do not require user accounts or registrations.

We may collect limited information that you voluntarily provide, such as when you contact us via email or a contact form.

2. How We Use Information

Any information you provide is used only to:

  • Maintain site security and reliability

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

ToolZenHub may use cookies or similar technologies to:

  • Understand how visitors interact with our tools
  • Improve user experience

We use third-party analytics services such as Google Analytics, which may collect non-personal information including browser type, device type, pages visited, and approximate location.

ToolZenHub may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners such as Google AdSense.

These partners may use cookies or web beacons to display relevant ads based on visits to this and other websites.

Users may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google’s Ads Settings.

5. Client-Side Processing

Most ToolZenHub tools process data directly in your browser. Input data is not uploaded, stored, or transmitted to our servers unless explicitly stated.

6. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those sites.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information from unauthorized access. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

8. Children’s Information

ToolZenHub does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe such information has been provided, please contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our practices, please contact us through our Contact page.
